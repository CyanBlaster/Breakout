Sprites made from pre-rendered high-poly spheres of different colors that imitate the bowling-ball-like objects found in Bob-Omb Battlefield in Super Mario 64.

These are suitable for use in games that replicate the low-definition style of Super Mario 64 or other Nintendo 64 games.

Each sphere is 64 x 64 pixels and deliberately has a sharp alpha mask for faithfulness to Super Mario 64.

These are quite "boring" assets but for those who are looking for faithfulness to the Nintendo 64 console they could be quite useful.

Made in Blender and Aseprite.
